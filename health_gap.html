Representation of women

In 2019 (2nd quarter), women accounted for 31% of parliament members in EU countries. The number of women in business leadership is low: in April 2019, women accounted for just 27.8% of board members of the largest publicly-listed companies registered in EU countries. The causes for the under-representation of women in decision-making processes and positions are multiple and complex. The main reasons are traditional gender roles and stereotypes, the lack of support for women and men to balance care responsibilities with work, and the prevalent political and corporate cultures.
